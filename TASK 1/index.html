why chose us

Travelling to another country to receive care can not just be overwhelming but also sound daunting. Keeping this in mind, we have therefore created a special concierge service to en-sure you and your family receive personalised care and cultural respect during a potentially difficult time in your life, in a place far from home.,

Our team goes the extra mile to not only provide you with technologically advanced world-class medical treatment but undivided attention from the moment you arrive at the airport. Its simple, we are there for you every step of the way. For more than half a century we have rev-olutionised patient care and patient experiences with expert doctors, advanced treatments, a plethora of surgical options, state-of-the-art infrastructure and an unparalleled commitment to clinical excellence — all under one roof. It has therefore come as no surprise that we have been conferred with the ‘The Best Medical Tourism Facility in India’ by the Government of India.

We have dedicatedly assisted patients from various parts of the globe such as Iraq, Iran, Oman, Yemen, Bahrain, UAE, Nigeria, Tanzania, Ethiopia, Kenya, Burundi, Uganda, Congo, Bangladesh, Afghanistan over the last few decades. Here at Ruby Hall Clinic, our patients comes first.
